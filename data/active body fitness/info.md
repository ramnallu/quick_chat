# Active Body Fitness

Active Body Fitness is a premier, results-driven fitness community based in East Windsor, NJ. Founded in 2019, we specialize in helping busy adults, parents, and professionals transform their bodies and minds through high-energy group fitness, personalized coaching, and 24/7 gym access.

## Our Mission & Vision
- **Vision:** To create an active and fit community that incorporates professional coaching principles to help people have a healthy lifestyle, feel better, and look great.
- **Mission:** To help members use resistance training methods to increase strength and build lean muscle with core exercises like squats, planks, presses, and deadlifts.

## Core Values
- **Earned, Not Given:** Results require work and focus.
- **Discipline Over Motivation:** Consistency is key; we show up regardless of how we feel.
- **No Excuses:** We take full responsibility for our actions and results.
- **Elevate Others:** Strength is about lifting those around you.
- **Be About Action:** We execute relentlessly on our goals.
- **Be Curious:** We ask, learn, and level up with intention.

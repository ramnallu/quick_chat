# Services & Programs

Active Body Fitness offers a comprehensive range of fitness solutions designed to fit the busy lifestyles of our members.

## 24/7 Gym Access
Get full access to our state-of-the-art facility whenever it fits your schedule.
- Modern cardio and strength equipment.
- Clean and well-maintained facilities.
- Secure phone-based door access for late-night or early-morning workouts.

## Group Training Classes
Our high-energy group classes are the heart of our community. We offer over 40 classes per week led by expert coaches.
- **Schedule:** Classes are Held Monday through Saturday.
- **Class Types:** Over 30 different class types targeting various body areas and fitness goals.
- **Levels:** Suitable for all levels, from beginners to experienced athletes. Movements are scaled to match your skill level.
- **Group Size:** Small group training with a maximum of 16 people per class for personalized attention.

## Personal Training
For those seeking individual attention, our certified personal trainers provide one-on-one coaching.
- **Custom Workout Plans:** Tailored specifically to your goals.
- **Nutrition Guidance:** Help with meal planning and healthy habits.
- **Progress Tracking:** Regular check-ins to ensure you're on the right path.
- **Flexible Scheduling:** Sessions booked at your convenience.

## Offers & Membership
- **First Class FREE:** Try any group fitness class at no cost (new members only).
- **Complimentary Consultation:** Free consultation for personal training.
- **No Contracts:** Join without long-term commitments; cancel anytime with no fees.

# White Tiger Martial Arts Academy - Overview

White Tiger Martial Arts Academy provides professional martial arts training in East Windsor, NJ, for students of all ages.

## About Us
- **Philosophy**: Combines traditional TaeKwonDo with practical self-defense.
- **Instructors**: Over 20 years of experience, bringing high-energy and stimulating classes.
- **Skills Developed**: Focus, concentration, discipline, listening, and hard work.

# Martial Arts Programs

We offer several programs tailored to different age groups and skill levels:

## Little Tigers (Ages 4-5)
- **Focus**: Introduction to basics, life skills development, focus, and concentration.
- **Goal**: Start young students on a path of discipline and physical activity.

## Juniors (Ages 6-12)
- **Focus**: Comprehensive learning experience covering movement, focus, discipline, and self-control.
- **System**: Follows the belt progression from White to Brown.

## Teen & Adult (Ages 12+)
- **Focus**: Complete martial arts experience, refining traditional styles and practical applications.
- **Environment**: High energy, stimulating, and respectful.
